#code of conduct
1. Honest and Ethical Conduct
This principle requires employees to act with the highest standards of personal and professional integrity.
It includes being free from fraud or deception and making decisions based on principles rather than personal interests. 
2. Compliance with Laws and Regulations
This ensures adherence to all applicable laws, rules, and regulations, including those related to safety, privacy, and financial reporting.
It also prohibits illegal practices like stealing or misrepresenting information. 
3. Confidentiality and Privacy
Employees must protect the company's confidential information, as well as the privacy of their colleagues, customers, and other stakeholders. 
This includes responsible use of company technology and data. 
4. Conflict of Interest Guidelines
This section defines what constitutes a conflict of interest and outlines how employees should handle situations where their personal interests might interfere with their professional duties.
It is crucial to avoid situations where the appearance of a conflict could damage the company's reputation. 
5. Reporting Violations
This establishes clear and confidential channels for employees to report any misconduct or violations of the code without fear of retaliation.
It ensures there are processes in place for investigating these reports and for outlining the consequences of violations.
